Tally Counter: Multi Clicker
Indragni Solutions

Tally Counter is a fast tally and clicker counter for iPhone and iPad: multiple counters, large +/− controls, optional notes, step size, goals, haptics, sounds, history with charts, and a Home Screen widget. Pro adds unlimited counters, no ads, CSV export, and extra sound packs.

Need more help? Email or contact options may be listed on indragnisolutions.com. When you write in, include your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce the issue; screenshots help.

Pro purchase and Restore

  • Pro is a one-time non-consumable purchase through Apple, not a subscription.
  • If Pro features or sound packs do not appear after purchase, open the App → Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Pro, and ensure you are online when restoring.

Home Screen widget

  • Add the widget: on the Home Screen, tap and hold → + → search for Tally Counter → add the widget.
  • Open the main app and use Edit (or the flow that assigns a counter) so the widget knows which counter to show and control.
  • If counts look stale, remove and re-add the widget, then confirm the assigned counter in the app.

Data and CSV (Pro)

  • Counters and history are stored on your device; there is no advertised automatic cloud sync.
  • With Pro, use Settings to export CSV for backup or spreadsheets before deleting the app or resetting the device.

Common questions

  • Is there a subscription? No — Pro is a single lifetime purchase.
  • Do I need an account? No account is required for core counting.
  • Ads? The free version may show ads; Pro removes them.
  • iPad? The app supports iPad; add the widget from the Home Screen on each device you use.

Privacy

For how we handle data (including ads in the free app), see the Privacy Policy for Tally Counter. The App Store listing also links to our published privacy URL when available.

Scope

Tally Counter is a manual utility: totals reflect the + and − actions you perform in the app or from the widget. It is not a medical device and does not measure biometrics.
