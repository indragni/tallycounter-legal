Privacy Policy

Tally Counter: Multi Clicker
Published by Indragni Solutions
Effective date: March 30, 2026

This policy describes how we handle information in connection with the iOS app Tally Counter: Multi Clicker (“the App”). If you use the App, you agree to this policy. We may update it; the effective date above will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

1. Summary

Your counter names, notes, values, goals, colors, and event history are stored on your device. We do not require an account to use core counting features. The free version may show ads and use standard app diagnostics as described below. Optional Pro features are purchased through Apple.

2. Information stored on your device

The App keeps your tally data locally on your iPhone or iPad (and in the app’s data container used by the Home Screen widget). This includes counters you create, increments and decrements, optional notes, sound preferences, and history used for charts. Unless you explicitly export or share content (for example, CSV export available with Pro), that data is not uploaded to our servers by the App for routine operation.

3. Advertising (free version)

The free tier may display advertisements through Google’s Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) or similar providers Apple allows. Ad networks may collect or receive identifiers and usage-related data to measure delivery, limit fraud, and personalize or limit ad frequency, according to their own policies and your device settings. You can learn more in Google’s privacy documentation and in your iOS Settings → Privacy choices (including App Tracking Transparency where applicable).

4. Diagnostics and performance

We may use platform or third-party tools that collect crash data, performance data, and limited usage or product interaction information to keep the App reliable and to understand how features are used. Where disclosed to Apple for the App Store, such data is described as not linked to your identity in our product’s privacy details; specifics depend on the SDKs enabled in a given build.

5. Purchases

Pro unlock and restores are processed by Apple In-App Purchase. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple provides us transaction identifiers needed to validate entitlement. Use Restore Purchases in the App’s Settings if you reinstall or change devices on the same Apple ID.

6. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13 to collect their personal information knowingly. If you believe we have inadvertently collected a child’s data, contact us via our support page and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Security and retention

On-device data remains until you delete the App or clear its data through iOS. Data held by Apple, ad partners, or analytics providers is retained according to their policies and legal requirements. Use a device passcode and current iOS versions to help protect your device.

8. Your choices

  • Upgrade to Pro to remove advertising from the App (subject to the current feature set).
  • Manage tracking and privacy settings in iOS Settings.
  • Delete the App to remove locally stored tallies (export first with Pro if you need a backup).

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests regarding this policy, contact us through our Support page or the contact options listed on indragnisolutions.com.

This document supports the App Store listing for Tally Counter: Multi Clicker. It does not replace Apple’s own terms or the privacy practices of Apple, Google, or other third-party SDKs integrated in the App.
